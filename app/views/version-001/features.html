Features

Services built with Submit can:

  • work with services that have less than 100,000 transactions per year
  • make simple calculations
  • use simple rules and logic
  • draw from static lists of data (that your service provides)
  • send information to a government organisation to process
  • let users save their progress and come back to it another time
  • send confirmation emails or text messages when users complete a transaction
  • enable individual users (not businesses) to prove their identity online
