Get help to create simple digital transactions

Use GOV.UK Submit to build and manage online forms with help from the Government Digital Service.

See how to get started

Build digital services without a digital team

With Submit, you can create simple online transactions without needing your own digital team. Instead, GDS will work with you and provide the tools and people you need.

You can build new forms, or create digital versions of ones you have offline.

Learn more about how to get started with Submit and the types of transaction you can currently create.

For services with fewer than 100,000 transactions

Some government services can’t justify investing in a full digital team.

Submit is designed for services with fewer than 100,000 transactions per year, across all government organisations.

Built for the needs of government

Submit is designed for the needs of government services and operational teams.

You’ll benefit from GDS’ experience in:

  • building transactions that are designed to meet user needs
  • the Digital Service Standard
  • GOV.UK style and accessibility standards

Better for users

Digital transactions can:

  • simplify complicated guidance and user journeys
  • automate simple calculations
  • accept online payments
  • offer assistance to users with accessibility needs

Better for services

Government services benefit from digital transactions, because they:

  • reduce the number of user errors
  • prevent ineligible applications
  • reduce the amount of support users need
  • save time spent on manual data entry
  • reduce paper processing
  • make it easier to manage payments
  • let you track and improve how your service is performing
