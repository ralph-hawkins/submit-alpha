Get help to create simple digital transactions

Use GOV.UK Submit to create and manage online transactions with help from the Government Digital Service.

Create an account or sign in if you’ve used it before.

Digital transactions without your own digital team

With Submit, you can create simple online transactions without needing your own digital team. Instead, GDS will work with you and provide the tools and people you need.

Learn more about how to get started with Submit and the types of transaction you can currently create.

All organisations, all services

Submit is designed for services of all sizes, across all government organisations.

The long tail of government services

Some services can’t justify a full digital transformation project because they:

  • don’t get enough transactions
  • can’t afford it
  • are in organisations that have other priorities

Built for the needs of government

Submit is built for government services. You can integrate it with your existing systems, and work with GDS designers and developers who are trained in the GOV.UK style and service standards.
