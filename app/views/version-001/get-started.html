Get started

With GOV.UK Submit, you can usually set up a new online transaction in a few weeks.

  1. Check Submit is right for your service

    We’re adding new functionality to Submit all the time.

    For now, you can check our features to find out whether your service is a good fit.

  2. Contact the Submit team

    Use our contact form to get in touch with us.

    Try to include details of what you’re hoping to build, and what existing forms (if any) you have.

    We’ll review your email and get back to you. If your transaction is a good fit for Submit, we’ll talk to you about setting up an initial visit.

  3. Meet with us to discuss your service

    We’ll meet with you to understand your service. We’ll talk about:

    • problems you or your users might have with the current service
    • your users and what they need from your service
    • any legal or policy constraints
    • who your stakeholders are
    • the business rules that govern your service

  4. Design and test your digital transaction

    We’ll work with you to design a user journey for your transaction.

    From this, we can then create a prototype to share with stakeholders and test with users.

    We’ll iterate the prototype based on feedback from this process.

  5. Go live
